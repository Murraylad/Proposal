Core Features

Everything you need to run your grounds and property maintenance business

Customer Management

Store and organize all customer information including residential, commercial, council, and housing association clients. Quick access to contact details, addresses, and notes.

Job Management

Create, schedule, and track all jobs from start to finish. Assign staff, set priorities, track progress, and manage service types across all your work.

Quotes & Invoicing

Generate professional quotes and invoices with your branding. Add line items, calculate VAT, include bank details, and send directly to customers via email.

Staff Scheduling

Assign team members to jobs, manage their calendars, and ensure the right people are in the right place at the right time.

Time Tracking

Employees can clock in and out from their mobile app. Track hours worked, view timesheets, and monitor attendance in real-time.

Team Messaging

Built-in messaging system for direct and group conversations. Keep your team connected without needing separate chat apps.

Push Notifications

Instant notifications on all devices when jobs are assigned, messages are received, or important updates occur.

Calendar View

See all jobs, meetings, and events in a clear calendar format. Plan ahead and never miss an appointment.
